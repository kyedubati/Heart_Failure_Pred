Variance Inflation Factor

In [7]:
from statsmodels.stats.outliers_influence import variance_inflation_factor
vif = pd.DataFrame()
numeric_ds = data.select_dtypes(['number'])
vif["features"] = numeric_ds.columns
vif["VIF"] = [variance_inflation_factor(numeric_ds.values, i) for i in range(numeric_ds.shape[1])]

VIF is high for restingBP, age, and MaxHR. It shows colinearity in those three aspects. As a result, we drop the highest VIF (RestingBP) and calculate the VIF.
